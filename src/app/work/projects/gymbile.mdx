## Overview

Gymbile is an all-in-one platform connecting personal trainers with clients worldwide. The platform enables live interactive sessions, flexible payments, and comprehensive client management tools.

## The Problem

Personal trainers face significant challenges in building and managing their online fitness business:
- Difficulty reaching clients beyond their local area
- Complex payment processing for international clients
- Lack of integrated tools for scheduling, communication, and progress tracking
- High platform fees eating into their earnings

## The Solution

Gymbile provides a complete ecosystem for fitness professionals:

- **Live Sessions**: Real-time interactive training sessions with HD video and audio
- **Client Management**: Track client progress, set goals, and manage workout plans
- **Flexible Payments**: Support for multiple currencies and payment methods
- **Scheduling**: Integrated calendar with timezone support for global clients

## Technology Stack

- **Backend**: Elixir/Phoenix for real-time features and scalability
- **Frontend**: React/Next.js for a responsive web experience
- **Real-time**: LiveKit for high-quality video sessions
- **Infrastructure**: AWS for reliable global deployment

## Market Opportunity

The digital health market represents a $96B addressable opportunity, with fitness tech being one of the fastest-growing segments post-pandemic.

## Current Status

Active development with early users onboarding. Focus on building the core platform and establishing product-market fit.
