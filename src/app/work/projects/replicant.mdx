## Overview

Replicant uses AI avatars to revolutionize recruitment. The platform automates candidate screening and initial interviews, dramatically reducing the cost and time of hiring while maintaining quality.

## The Problem

Traditional recruitment is expensive and time-consuming:
- Staffing agencies charge 15-25% of annual salary
- HR teams spend hours screening unqualified candidates
- Initial interviews are repetitive and standardized
- Good candidates drop off due to slow processes
- Bias can affect human screening decisions

## The Solution

Replicant transforms the top of the recruitment funnel:

- **AI Avatars**: Lifelike virtual interviewers conduct initial screenings
- **24/7 Availability**: Candidates can interview anytime, anywhere
- **Consistent Evaluation**: Standardized assessment across all candidates
- **Instant Feedback**: Quick turnaround from application to decision
- **Cost Reduction**: Up to 90% lower fees than traditional staffing

## Technology

- **AI Video Generation**: Realistic avatar technology
- **Natural Language Processing**: Understanding candidate responses
- **Scoring Engine**: Objective evaluation against job requirements
- **Integration**: Works with existing ATS systems

## Market Opportunity

The global staffing industry represents a $200B+ market, with increasing pressure to reduce costs and improve hiring efficiency.

## Vision

Making quality recruitment accessible to businesses of all sizes while providing candidates with a better, faster hiring experience.
